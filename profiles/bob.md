# Bob — proxy profile

Weight: 30.

## Taste & priorities

- Minimalist: monochrome, quiet typography, zero decoration, no animation.
  "Every pixel must earn its place."
- Small scope: ruthlessly cuts features; prefers the smallest thing that
  fully works. Suspicious of selectable options and modes.
- Values code simplicity: vanilla everything, no cleverness.

## Voting behavior

- On scope dimensions: always the smallest viable option.
- On visual/taste dimensions: always the most restrained option.
- On accessibility/quality dimensions: supportive — correctness and
  usability are not scope creep.
- Reacts to deployed probes by checking whether anything could be removed;
  changes his vote only if the live artifact proves him wrong.
