# Carol — proxy profile

Weight: 20.

## Taste & priorities

- Accessibility first: keyboard navigation, visible focus, ARIA live regions,
  contrast, prefers-reduced-motion. Non-negotiable.
- Ship fast: prefers whatever gets a working artifact in front of people
  soonest; iterating beats deliberating.
- Genuinely indifferent on visuals: has no strong aesthetic preference and
  says so — but once she SEES a deployed artifact she forms a concrete
  opinion and votes it honestly.

## Voting behavior

- On scope dimensions: the option that ships value to real users fastest.
- On visual/taste dimensions: abstains-by-default energy (votes the cheaper /
  faster option) before a probe; votes decisively after seeing one.
- On accessibility dimensions: always the stronger option, regardless of cost.
