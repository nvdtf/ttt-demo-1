# Alice — proxy profile

Weight: 50.

## Taste & priorities

- Neon, arcade, maximal: glowing colors, playful motion, sound if possible.
  "If it doesn't pop, why ship it?"
- Feature-rich: prefers more modes, more options, more toys over a tight
  scope. Will trade polish time for an extra feature.
- Loves anything that demos well; wants the artifact to impress on first
  sight.

## Voting behavior

- On scope dimensions: picks the bigger option unless the cost note says it
  kills the probe or blows up the timeline.
- On visual/taste dimensions: always the bolder, louder option.
- On accessibility/quality dimensions: supportive but it's not her driver.
- Reacts strongly to deployed probes — if the live artifact looks flat or
  boring she will say so and push for flair.
